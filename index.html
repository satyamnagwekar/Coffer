About the Project
Gold has always been India's most trusted store of wealth. But nobody had built a clean way to know what it's actually worth.
Indian families hold an estimated 25,000 tonnes of physical gold*, the largest private gold reserve in the world. Yet the tools to track it barely existed. Spreadsheets, rough mental estimates, or simply not knowing at all.
The cruellest part: the only reliable way to find out what your gold was worth was to sell it. Accept a jeweller's price, and only then discover the number by giving up the very thing you wanted to hold.
Meanwhile, anyone who moved their gold into ETFs for the sake of a clean P&L found themselves holding a digital promise instead of the real thing, bought at today's prices, not the prices their physical gold was acquired at over decades.
And when it comes to succession, the problem compounds. Gold passed down through families often exists without documentation, without valuation, and without a clear record for probate or a will. Heirs are left guessing what was held, where, and what it was worth.
MyAurum was built for all three problems. It's a private digital ledger for anyone who owns physical gold or silver, jewellery, coins, bars, raw metal, and wants to know what their holdings are worth right now, in real rupees, with a full gain and loss picture. A record that exists, is current, and can travel with a will.
Prices are sourced from global spot markets and aligned to MCX rates for Indian users, updated every five minutes. Your data is stored on encrypted servers and never shared with anyone.
*World Gold Council estimate
The Founder
S
Satyam Nagwekar
Founder, MyAurum
Satyam has spent his career in financial journalism, content, and marketing, writing about markets, money, and the economic forces that shape everyday life in India. His father accumulated gold over decades the way most Indian families do: mini gold coins, silver articles, jewellery received as gifts. Not investments. Just savings, quietly held.
A few years ago Satyam sold most of it and bought gold ETFs instead. This was driven by a desire to get a conveniently trackable P&L picture and eliminate ambiguity. The logic was sound. The price was not. Those pieces had been bought over decades at costs he will never see again. The ETFs that replaced them cost significantly more per gram. He had converted low-cost physical gold into high-cost digital gold and called it a sensible decision. The pieces were gone.
MyAurum is the tool he wishes had existed before that decision. Built for anyone who holds physical metal and wants to know what it is worth today, without having to sell it to find out.

Privacy Policy

What we collect
MyAurum stores only what you choose to enter: your name, email, and the holding details you add. We do not collect behavioural data, track usage patterns, or build profiles.

Where it lives
Your holdings data is stored on your device in your browser's local storage. Only your account credentials (name, email, password hash) are held on our servers — nothing about your portfolio, its contents, values, or history is ever transmitted to any server or third party.

No ads, ever
MyAurum is free to use and carries no advertising. Your financial data will never be sold, shared, or used to target you. The product exists to serve you — not to monetise your information.

Third parties
We use a third-party market data feed solely to retrieve live gold and silver spot prices. No personal data is transmitted to this service. No other third-party services receive your data.

Data deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile tab. Holdings stored in your browser are erased when you clear your browser data or uninstall the app.
