About the Project
Your gold has a story. Now it has an enduring record
Indian families hold an estimated 25,000 tonnes of physical gold*, the largest private gold reserve in the world. Much of it was bought over decades — at prices that will never come again. It travels with families to Dubai, London, Toronto, Sydney. Jewellery inherited, coins tucked away, bars bought quietly. Not investments on a screen. Savings in metal.
The cruellest part: the only reliable way to find out what your gold was worth was to sell it. Accept a jeweller's price, and only then discover the number — by giving up the very thing you wanted to hold.
Meanwhile, anyone who moved their gold into ETFs for the sake of a clean P&L found themselves holding a digital promise instead of the real thing, bought at today's prices, not the prices their physical gold was acquired at over decades.
And when it comes to succession, the problem compounds. Gold passed down through families often exists without documentation, without valuation, and without a clear record for probate or a will. Heirs are left guessing what was held, where, and what it was worth.
MyAurum was built for all three problems. It's a private digital ledger for anyone who owns physical gold or silver — jewellery, coins, bars, raw metal — and wants to know what their holdings are worth right now, in their currency, with a full gain and loss picture. A record that exists, is current, and can travel with a will. Prices update every five minutes from global spot markets, with MCX-aligned rates for India. Supports rupees, dollars, dirhams, and pounds.
Your data is stored on encrypted servers and never shared with anyone.
*World Gold Council estimate

How your data is protected

Your password is hashed using bcrypt (cost factor 12) and is never stored in readable form — not even we can retrieve it. Sensitive holding fields (item names, purity grades, notes, held-by names, nominee names) are encrypted at the application layer using AES-256-GCM before being written to the database. Each field uses a unique random initialisation vector, and the authentication tag prevents tampering. Even with direct database access, this data cannot be read without the encryption key, which is stored separately.

All communication between your device and our servers uses TLS. Our database runs on Railway's infrastructure backed by AWS, where storage volumes are encrypted at rest by default.

Login endpoints are rate-limited to mitigate brute-force attempts. Session tokens (JWTs) expire after 30 days. Optional two-factor authentication via email OTP is available in Settings for users who want an additional login verification step.

Privacy Policy

1. What we collect
MyAurum stores only what you choose to enter: your name, email address, age, country, and the holding details you add — item names, metal type, purity, weight, purchase price, purchase date, nominated heirs, and any notes you attach. We do not collect behavioural analytics, device fingerprints, location data, or usage patterns. We do not build profiles. There is no advertising SDK, no tracking pixel, and no third-party analytics running in this application.

2. Where your data lives
Your account and all holdings data are stored on a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway's infrastructure, which runs on Amazon Web Services (AWS). AWS encrypts storage volumes at rest by default. Data in transit between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. We do not store anything in your browser's local storage or on your device beyond a session token.

3. How we protect your data
Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12 — they are never stored in readable form, and we cannot retrieve your password. Sensitive holding fields — item names, purity grades, notes, held-by names, nominee names — are encrypted at the application layer using AES-256-GCM before being written to the database. This means that even direct database access would not expose this information in readable form without the encryption key, which is stored separately from the data. Authentication tokens (JWTs) expire after 30 days. Login endpoints are rate-limited to mitigate brute-force attempts. Optional two-factor authentication via email OTP is available for users who want an additional layer of login security.

4. No ads. No data sharing. Ever.
MyAurum carries no advertising. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for commercial purposes. We do not use your financial data to target you with anything. The product exists to serve you — not to monetise your information. This is a categorical commitment, not a policy subject to revision for commercial convenience.

5. Third-party services
We use the following external services, each with a narrow and specific purpose:

  • Metals price APIs — to retrieve live gold, silver, and platinum spot prices. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Resend — to deliver transactional emails (verification, password reset, weekly digest). Your email address is transmitted to Resend solely for delivery. Resend does not receive any holdings data.
  • Google OAuth — if you choose to sign in with Google. We receive only your name and email address. No data is shared back with Google.
  • Railway / AWS — infrastructure hosting. Your data resides on their servers subject to their security standards.
  • Sentry — for application error monitoring. Error reports contain technical stack traces and may include sanitised request metadata, but never your holdings data or personal identifiers.

6. Email communications
If you provide an email address, we may send transactional emails: account verification, password resets, a milestone notification at 20 holdings, and an optional weekly portfolio digest. The weekly digest can be disabled at any time via the unsubscribe link in each email. We do not send promotional or marketing emails.

7. Data retention and deletion
Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Settings → Danger Zone. Deletion is immediate and irreversible. Backups are retained for up to 30 days on Railway's infrastructure before expiring.

8. No KYC. No financial regulation.
MyAurum is a personal record-keeping tool. It is not a financial product, a brokerage, a payment service, or a regulated financial instrument. We do not require identity documents, PAN, Aadhaar, or any government-issued identification. We have no visibility into your financial accounts and no ability to initiate transactions on your behalf.

9. Children
MyAurum is not intended for use by persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to this policy
If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify users by email and update the date below. We will not retroactively reduce the privacy protections described here without explicit consent.

11. Contact
For privacy-related questions or data requests, write to us at [email protected]. We will respond within 5 business days.
